## Ask Around

The first tip on how to find an SEO agency is the most straightforward one: ask around. Paying for SEO is a major investment in the visibility of your business, so you don't want to skimp on the research.

If you're narrowing down your SEO agency options but don't have reviews, ask them for references. The most reputable SEO agencies should have reviews from past clients. Don't forget to [ask for referrals](https://review42.com/resources/referral-marketing-statistics/) from your colleagues, too.

Getting this kind of input from professionals who know how SEO works will save you time and money in the long run.

## Ask for Proof

You're figuring out how to choose an SEO agency, and you narrow down your options. Let's say one SEO agency has great reviews. Maybe you've even gotten a referrable from an expert.

Next, ask your SEO agency options for a track record. They should be able to show you tangible results of their efforts. You can look at specific examples of their work to test their skill or ask for a case study.

## Know What You Want

Even if you're not too clear on how SEO works, it's still important that you outline what you want from an SEO agency. What are your goals for the year? How will SEO help you get to the next level?

Express these goals with SEO agency options. They should be able to create a digital marketing strategy that helps you meet these goals.

## Determine Your Budget

Before comparing SEO service costs, know what you want to pay. Remember, you don't have to spend big to get the best SEO agency in Colorado Springs. You can still get quality SEO services at a decent price if you do your research.

If you have a small budget, you can also fill in the gaps by learning SEO on your own. Then, you can try the [Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com/analytics/web/provision/#/provision) tool, which is free.

## Write Up a Contract

You finally land on the best SEO agency in Colorado Springs. Now, it's time to detail exactly what's going into the contract. Be sure to answer these questions:

1.  How much does this cost?
2.  When are we due for payment?
3.  What are the exact services we are receiving from this SEO agency?
