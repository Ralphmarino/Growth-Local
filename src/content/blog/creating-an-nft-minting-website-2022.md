Do you want to build an NFT minting website to promote your project, but aren’t sure where to start?

There’s a lot that goes into launching a successful NFT project, but the launch itself is just the beginning. You also need to know how to make an NFT minting website to promote and run your project.

Fortunately, we have you covered with these five tips on [NFT website design](https://growthlocal.com/services/web-development/). Keep reading to learn what goes into a great NFT site.

## 1\. Explain Your “Why”

NFTs are a revolutionary new technology, which is reason enough to invest in them. However, there are a lot of competing NFT projects on the market. How does your stand out?

The first section of your homepage should explain that to your visitors. Demonstrate the [passion behind your project](https://www.fastcompany.com/90711781/your-people-want-purpose-take-these-3-steps-to-define-your-why), not just the technology.

Why are you selling NFTs? What does your project stand for?

## 2\. Make Yourself Understood

Because the concept of NFTs is so cutting-edge, many people have questions about what exactly they are. You can clear the air and relieve any apprehensions they might have against buying by explaining how your project works.

It’s a good idea to go into the basics of what NFTs are and how they work. Then, describe your intended roadmap for your particular project.

## 3\. Make it Easy to Buy

Actually being able to mint NFTs is the point of an NFT website, so make it easy for interested investors to buy. Tell them how much each NFT costs, what blockchain they are on, and what sales limits apply.

## 4\. Emphasize the Art

People have lots of reasons for buying NFTs. Some people see them as great investment opportunities while others simply love being a part of something new.

But perhaps the biggest reason people buy NFTs is that they like the art itself. So be sure to showcase the art you’re using in your project. Let potential buyers see what they’ll be getting when they buy one of your NFTs.

## 5\. Prioritize Security

Security is a big priority anytime you’re dealing with new technology (or the internet in general, for that matter). For that reason, you’ll want to make sure the security and safety of your website are top-tier.

Make sure you’re working with a reputable hosting platform with support for NFT minting. Use HTTPS and get an SSL certificate when you sign up. And make sure you understand how [NFTs and blockchain technology](https://www.theverge.com/22310188/nft-explainer-what-is-blockchain-crypto-art-faq) work before you start.
