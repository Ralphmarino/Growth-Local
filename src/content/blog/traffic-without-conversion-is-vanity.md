It's easy to celebrate a traffic chart that goes up and to the right. But traffic
is a means, not an end. If those visitors don't become leads, the chart is a
vanity metric in a nicer outfit.

## SEO and CRO are one system

Search earns the visit. **Conversion Rate Optimization** earns the outcome. Treat
them separately and you'll pour budget into ranking for terms that never turn
into revenue. Treat them as one funnel and every ranking gain compounds.

## What we look at first

- **Message match.** Does the page deliver on the promise of the query that
  brought someone there?
- **Friction.** Every extra field, unclear label, or slow step is a place people
  quietly leave.
- **Proof.** Trust signals — results, testimonials, specifics — placed where
  hesitation actually happens.

## Test, don't guess

Opinions are cheap; behavior is the truth. We run structured experiments against
real traffic, keep what wins, and feed the learning back into both the page and
the keyword strategy.

The goal was never rankings. It was growth you can put in the bank.
