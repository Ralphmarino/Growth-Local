You’re building a new website. You’re excited and nervous at the same time. What comes next? What new website tips should you follow? You might be thinking, “how do I start SEO for my new website?

If you haven’t started looking into search engine optimization (SEO), now is a great time to read an SEO guide filled with tips for starting a new website.

It’s never too early to start thinking about SEO. In the article below, you’ll find some information on your plan to start launching new website SEO strategies. Read on to learn more about optimizing your site for search.

## Think About Site Structure When Creating Your Website

When you think about SEO, you probably think about keywords and links. Did you know that [user experience](https://searchengineland.com/seo-ux-success-286638#:~:text=Think%20of%20the%20partnership%20of,both%20SEO%20and%20user%20experience.) (UX) is another important factor in your search ranking? One of the best SEO tips for new website architecture is to make sure the site is easy to use.

You should have navigation bars that are easy to find. It may also be smart to have a few people try to use your website and provide you with feedback. Google tends to reward websites that have a high usability score, so keep UX in mind when you’re designing the site.

## Start Building Your Content Calendar

If you ask to have [SEO explained](https://growthlocal.com/local-seo/) to you by an expert, you’ll probably hear about the importance of fresh, original content.

When you add new content to your website, you give Google a reason to crawl your site to find new pages and keywords.

The more Google crawls your site, the more likely it is that your site moves up in the search rankings. There’s no better time to start building your content calendar than when you’re first designing the site.

Think about what type of articles you’d like to write, which keywords you’re going to target, and how often you’ll be able to post new content on the website. Content will be one of your main website marketing tactics.

## Get Familiar With SEO Tools

Software tools like [Google Analytics](https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/analytics/), [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/), [SEMrush](https://www.semrush.com/) and [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/about) can help you measure the success of your SEO strategy. Google Analytics will help you see how much traffic you’re getting from organic search results.

It will also show you which pages on your website drive the most organic traffic. The other tools will help you find keywords to target with [your SEO strategy](https://growthlocal.com/blog/what-is-seo/).
