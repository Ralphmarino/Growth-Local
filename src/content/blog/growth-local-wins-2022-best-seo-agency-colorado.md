#### _Growth Local has been recognized as the best SEO Agency and Web Development company in Colorado._

**Colorado Springs, July 25, 2022.**

Growth Local, a Colorado Springs-based digital marketing and web development company formed in 2020 has been named the best SEO and Web Development Company in Colorado for 2022.

The prestigious award was announced by Acquisition International, an international monthly digital magazine with a circulation of over 85,000 professionals, after reviewing the competition in the Research and Development portion of its contests. Growth Local was picked because of its ability to research and understand consumer behavior, especially during the global pandemic. The company achieved this recognition by focusing on developing effective online strategies and web designs for local, national, international, and eCommerce markets. It achieves success by performing specific services including:

-   **Keyword Analysis**. This includes researching keywords, phrases and queries that potential customers use to locate specific services and goods.
-   **Keyword Mapping**. Assigns or maps the keywords to specific pages a consumer is interested in reading on a customer’s website.
-   **Google Business Profile Optimization**. This ensures customer websites are properly listed and optimized on Google search engines.
-   **Competitor Analysis**. Studies competitors and what they are doing to attract customers while developing strategies to do it better.
-   **Blog Content**. Blog content is essential to incorporating keywords and phrases to attract customers.

"_After much deliberation, we are delighted to inform you that Growth Local has been successful and named: Best SEO Agency & Web Development Company - Colorado. We hope this accolade demonstrates how valued your contribution to the global business landscape is by Acquisition International_." - Laura Hunter (Awards Manager) – Acquisition International AI Global Media
